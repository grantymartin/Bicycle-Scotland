About:

Hello and welcome to my website which brings together a combination of all the things I love to do to keep myself occupied.

When I go exploring on my bike, I call these days my "healing days". These are days where I get a chance to unclutter my mind, reduce any tensions or stress and by particularly paying attention to what I see around me on those rides, I know I am actively looking after both my physical and mental health in equal measures.

I am not a professional tour guide, photographer, cyclist, web designer or writer. I simply get huge enjoyment from exploring Scotland, undertaking research and reflecting through the power of words, the rides that I have undertaken.

As someone who is now in their mid fifties, I don't mind admitting to riding an e-bike. A bike that "electrically assists" my pedalling when I require it has given me so much more freedom and allows me to undertake tours and rides that I would normally shy away from. In fact, my fitness has improved so much that I now regularly ride my road bike as well!

The length of time I spend on a bike or distance travelled is not that important to me, I simply want to step off my bike at the end of a ride knowing that I have enjoyed myself and hopefully educated myself a little bit along the way.

I hope you enjoy the content of my website and if you have any great ideas, let me know, there is so much to explore and learn and so many more "healing days" to go out exploring.

Stewart Martin.
